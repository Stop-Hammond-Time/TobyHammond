6.4 Apply professional learning and improve student learning

This is an image of a sensory seating cushion I gave to a student with ASD after completing a course with Positive Partnerships. I also offer him the use of a fidget and a choice in seating (either on his cushion or a chair) as I learnt from the course that these strategies often help students with ASD regulate and focus. It has helped immensely with his engagement
