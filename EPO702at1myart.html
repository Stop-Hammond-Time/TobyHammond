My Artwork

My artwork takes the form of a short video that documents my morning commute on my last placement. It is shot from the perspective of the rider and has a voice over sharing my thoughts and feelings so that the audience can better experience the ride as I experience it.

It demonstrates the pleasure that can come from human powered transport and aims to inspire others to choose alternate forms of transport over cars. It highlights the positive effect on both air pollution, wellbeing and connection with one's community through human powered transport. It also highlights some of the barriers to cycling. Most barriers to cycling have to do with feeling unsafe due to proximity to cars and motorists who either lack the knowledge or respect for cyclists. The video draws attention to these to raise public awareness that will aid in improved infrastructure, improved disposition towards cyclists and sharing knowledge of road laws that keep all road users safe.

In this video, I demonstrate the ease of this commute afforded by some excellent bicycle infrastructure. A wide shared path, removed from the road makes travelling feel safe for both riders and pedestrians, parkland provides some beautiful scenery and an easy stop at the bakery for some breakfast is a treat that makes me feel connected to my community.
