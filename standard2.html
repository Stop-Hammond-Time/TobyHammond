2.4 Understand and respect Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people to promote reconciliation between Indigenous and non-Indigenous Australians

My evidence includes excerpts from my EEE755 (Numeracy and Social Justice) essay and my HD result. In this assessment, I outlined the history and ongoing impacts of colonisation on Indigenous Australians. This demonstrates my deep understanding of social justice issues and the importance of acknowledging Indigenous knowledge and the continued effects of colonialism (see fig. 4).

2.6 Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

During my placement, I used AI to create personalised "choose your own adventure" role-play stories, tailored to students' reading levels and interests. Students engaged by reading and responding to the story, either typing or verbally communicating their decisions. This approach significantly increased engagement, especially for students who were typically disengaged in literacy activities.
