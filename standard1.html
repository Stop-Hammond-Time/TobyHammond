1.6 Strategies to support full participation of students with disability

For one particular student with ASD and a learning disability, I differentiated his task that was centred around creating a map of the school and using co-ordinates, grids and directions. I instead allowed them to create a map from a fantasy story they had created with AI for a different task (similar to my evidence for 2.6). This was met with significantly increased engagement and learning and still met the learning goals of using grid co-ordinates and following direction.
